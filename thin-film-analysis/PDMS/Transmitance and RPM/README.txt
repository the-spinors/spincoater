Description
-----------
PDMS from before 05-09-24 (non-inclusive) were made with too much PDMS and too little time,
so interference pattern is not clear.
We made some tests on 05-09-24 with less PDMS and hold time at 1.5 min / 2 min.
Films on the 11-09-24 represent the first succesful batch of films, with hold times at 1.5 min and
transmitances analyzed at normal incidence.
Films on the 19-09-24 are also succesful. Hold time: 2 min, normal incidence.
On the 24-09-24 we reanalyzed films from the 11-09-24 and 19-09-24 at oblique incidence. Results
are coherent with those obtained at normal incidence.
